MR Elastrography estimates of hepatic stiffness (at 60 Hz correlate with hepatic

fibrosis stages as :

< 2.5 kPa : Normal.

2.5 - 2.9 kPa : Normal or Inflammation.

2.9 - 3.5 kPa : Stage 1 to 2 Fibrosis.

3.5 - 4 kPa : Stage 2 to 3 Fibrosis.

4 - 5 kPa : Stage 3 to 4 Fibrosis.

>5 kPa : Stage 4 Fibrosis or Cirrhosis.

(These are broad catogeries and results should be interpreted with

clinical and laboratory findings and for other possible causes of increased

liver stiffness).
